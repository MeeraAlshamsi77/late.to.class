Late to Class

A Short Film by MMM Productions

Welcome to the official website for the movie premiere of Late to Class! This short film combines laughter, suspense, and the relatable struggles of three students who always seem to find themselves running late for class in the most unexpected and hilarious ways. From chaotic mornings to surprising twists, the story captures the humor, friendship, and challenges of student life, all while keeping you on the edge of your seat.

Filmed on the breathtaking NYU Abu Dhabi campus, the movie also serves as a love letter to university life; showcasing the architecture, energy, and spirit of the campus that became the perfect backdrop for the story. Every shot is designed to make viewers feel as though they are running through the hallways and courtyards with the characters, experiencing the rush of being late first-hand.

Stay tuned for premiere details, behind-the-scenes content, and special interviews with the cast and crew. Whether you're a student yourself or simply love a good coming-of-age story, this film promises a heartwarming and unforgettable viewing experience.
