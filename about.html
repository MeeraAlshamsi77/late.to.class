Late to Class is a short film produced by MMM Productions. It follows the hilarious and sometimes heartwarming story of three students who find themselves running late for class in the most unexpected ways.

The film blends comedy with real life themes about friendship, responsibility, and the challenges of student life. It shows how even the smallest moments can turn into unforgettable adventures.

Directed and produced locally, Late to Class is a project that highlights creativity, teamwork, and the spirit of storytelling. It is not just a film, but also a reminder that sometimes our biggest struggles can turn into our best memories.

Filmed on the NYU Abu Dhabi campus, Late to Class transforms familiar spaces into the backdrop of a thrilling story. From bustling classrooms to quiet courtyards, every scene was carefully shot to highlight the vibrant atmosphere of the university. The film doesn't just use NYUAD as a set; it celebrates it, turning the campus into a character of its own. Viewers will recognize iconic spots and discover hidden corners, making the story feel both relatable and cinematic for students, faculty, and anyone who has experienced life on campus.
