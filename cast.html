The film Late to Class stars three talented actors who bring the story to life with their energy and creativity. Each cast member adds their own unique spark to the film, making it fun, exciting, and unforgettable.

  • Meera plays the determined but clumsy student who always tries to stay organized but ends up in the funniest situations.
  • Mouza takes on the role of the bright friend who always has clever and sometimes silly solutions to every problem.
  • Mahra plays the calm and thoughtful student who tries to keep everyone on track, but often gets dragged into the chaos.

Together, they form the perfect trio of friends whose journey will keep the audience entertained from beginning to end.
